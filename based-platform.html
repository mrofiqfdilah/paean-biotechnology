Mitochondrial Transplatation

MitoTherapy Products are novel medicinal products using mitochondria isolated from human stem cells or human blood. Mitochondria are unique organelles that are responsible for energy production in cells. Based on our experimental results, mitochondria have a high potential to treat inflammatory diseases.

Mitochondria Uptake in Cells

Mitochondria, when introduced into a cell from the outside, can be actively internalized through a process called macropinocytosis, allowing them to integrate and influence cellular function.

Advantages of Mitochondrial Transplantation

Unlocking Cellular Recovery Through Advanced MitoTherapy

Increasing ATP Production

Transplanted mitochondria help restore the cell’s energy powerhouse, significantly enhancing ATP levels vital for cell survival and function.

No Immunogenicity

As mitochondria can be autologous (sourced from the patient), they are naturally accepted by the immune system, minimizing rejection risk.

Improving Mitochondrial Function

Healthy mitochondria replace or support damaged ones, revitalizing cellular metabolism and restoring critical biochemical pathways.

Attenuating ROS Level and Inflammation

Mitochondrial transfer helps reduce oxidative stress and inflammation by lowering reactive oxygen species (ROS) levels in damaged cells.

Preparing Mitochondria for Therapeutic Use

Stem Cells Banking

High-quality stem cells are securely preserved under optimal conditions. This ensures availability for future therapeutic applications.
